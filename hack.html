About Us




The COVID-19 pandemic is raging in India and people are dying for the sheer lack of medical oxygen. The supply of which is available, but for which transportation and distribution is the main challenge.


The organization is a collective of public health experts ,volunteers, and concerned citizens around the india. This initiative would not be possible without our dedicated volunteers.


Aim

Our long time Goal is Empower the country to face future crisis by building a network of PSA plants, oxygen cylinders, and concentrators to augment oxygen supplies in India.
